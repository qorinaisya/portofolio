Mystery Shopper Study – Walk-In Banking Channel

Context: A financial institution aimed to evaluate the quality and consistency of service delivery across its walk-in banking channels, ensuring frontline staff met service standards and delivered a positive customer experience.

Solution: Led a Mystery Shopper program across multiple branches:

  • Assessed customer experience from the lens of everyday walk-in clients
  • Evaluated key touchpoints: greeting, service speed, staff knowledge, problem-solving, and closure
  • Benchmarked performance against internal KPIs and industry service standards
  • Provided detailed branch-level reports and actionable feedback loops

Impact:

  • Identified service gaps and inconsistencies across branches
  • Provided training and development inputs to improve staff behavior and service delivery
  • Helped reinforce a customer-first culture through real, unbiased experience tracking
  • Supported internal performance management and branch excellence programs
