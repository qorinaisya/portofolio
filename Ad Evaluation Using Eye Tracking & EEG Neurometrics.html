Ad Evaluation Using Eye Tracking & EEG Neurometrics

Category: FMCG

Context: A new brand was preparing to launch a high-virality ad campaign to position itself as a strong challenger to the market leader. To ensure the creative execution resonated, we needed to evaluate both conscious feedback and subconscious reactions from the audience.

Solution:

  • Eye Tracking: Captured heatmaps showing exactly where attention landed and for how long—frame by frame.
  • EEG: Measured real-time emotional states like engagement, confusion, and boredom every split second.
  • Qualitative Deep-Dive: Follow-up interviews uncovered the “why” behind reactions and guided creative improvements.

This hybrid method gave a full picture—what people noticed, felt, and remembered.

Impact:

  • Drove creative refinements that made the ad more emotionally engaging and visually clear
  • Final version achieved higher brand recall and significantly increased awareness at launch
  • Helped establish the brand as a serious competitor in the market
  • Provided a tested methodology for future campaign evaluations
