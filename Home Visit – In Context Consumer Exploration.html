Home Visit – In Context Consumer Exploration

Context: To uncover deeper behavioral and emotional insights, we conducted home visits to observe consumers in their natural environments—providing a richer understanding beyond what traditional surveys could offer.

Solution: Designed and led in-home ethnographic sessions, focusing on:

  • Observing everyday routines, product usage, and decision-making contexts
  • Conducting in-depth interviews to explore attitudes, motivations, and unmet needs
  • Capturing contextual cues like storage habits, brand visibility, and real-life product interaction
  • Using visual and audio documentation to enhance storytelling and insight delivery

Impact:

  • Revealed authentic pain points and unmet needs not surfaced in quantitative research
  • Informed product innovation and marketing strategies with real-life context
  • Helped teams build stronger consumer empathy and relevance in product design
  • Translated insights into personas, journey maps, and inspiration for campaign development
