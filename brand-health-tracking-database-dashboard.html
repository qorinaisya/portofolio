Brand Health Tracking Database and Dashboard

Context: Brand Health Tracking reports were often delayed due to manual processes and scattered data sources, limiting timely decision-making for the business.

Solution: Led the development of a centralized BHT database and an automated dashboard to streamline data access and reporting.

Key Features:

  • Real-time brand performance monitoring
  • Trend tracking across awareness, usage, conversion, and loyalty metrics
  • Easy filtering by category, demographic, and geography

Impact:

  • Reduced reporting delays from weeks to minutes
  • Enabled faster, insight-driven decisions across teams
  • Improved data accessibility for brand, marketing, and insights teams
  • Standardized reporting for better consistency and alignment
