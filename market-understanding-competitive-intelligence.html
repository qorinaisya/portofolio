Market Understanding – Competitive Intelligence

Context: To strengthen business strategy and innovation planning, there was a need to gain a deep understanding of market dynamics, competitor movements, and consumer shifts within a highly competitive category.

Solution: Led a competitive intelligence and market landscape study, focusing on:

  • Mapping category trends, growth drivers, and pain points
  • Analyzing key competitor strategies across pricing, communication, distribution, and innovation
  • Identifying white space opportunities through gap and need-state analysis
  • Synthesizing data from multiple sources—consumer research, desk research, and expert interviews

Impact:

  • Delivered a clear picture of market positioning and strategic moves
  • Enabled the brand to sharpen its competitive edge through differentiated messaging and offerings
  • Informed go-to-market strategies and pipeline innovation aligned with unmet needs
  • Supported cross-functional planning with actionable insights on market threats and opportunities
