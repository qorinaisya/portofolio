Needscope Segmentation Study

Context: A brand needed to clarify its emotional positioning and unlock growth opportunities in a cluttered category. The goal was to define distinct consumer segments and understand their underlying emotional drivers.

Solution: Led a segmentation study using the NeedScope framework, which combines psychology-based modeling and semiotic analysis to uncover:

  • Emotional need states that drive brand choice
  • Distinct consumer segments based on motivations, attitudes, and behaviors
  • Strategic fit between brand personality and target segments

Impact:

  • Identified clear white space for emotional positioning and communication
  • Helped the brand refine its tone, imagery, and messaging to better connect with high-potential segments
  • Informed portfolio and innovation strategy by aligning product offerings with segment needs
  • Enabled more targeted marketing and creative briefs for stronger brand relevance and differentiation
