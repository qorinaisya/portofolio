Campaign Post Evaluation

Context: Following the launch of a major campaign, there was a need to evaluate its overall performance, understand how well it delivered on key communication objectives, and uncover optimization points for future campaigns.

Solution: Led a comprehensive post-evaluation study combining both quantitative and qualitative approaches:

  • Measured awareness uplift, message recall, and brand linkage
  • Assessed creative impact across different audience segments
  • Analyzed channel effectiveness to understand where the campaign performed best
  • Conducted qualitative interviews to deep-dive into emotional resonance, clarity, and relevance

Impact:

  • Provided clear visibility on what worked and what didn’t—across media, message, and market
  • Informed future media planning and content strategy with data-backed recommendations
  • Helped optimize creative executions for better engagement and relevance
  • Strengthened campaign effectiveness tracking across marketing and brand teams
