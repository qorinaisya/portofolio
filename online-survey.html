Online Survey

Context: To support quick business decisions—including brand naming, messaging, and other tactical choices—we needed an agile research method that could deliver fast, reliable consumer input.

Solution: Led the design and execution of multiple online surveys targeting relevant consumer segments:

  • Tested brand name options, gauging appeal, relevance, and memorability
  • Explored simple business questions (e.g., concept preference, communication clarity, packaging cues)
  • Applied automated routing, real-time data collection, and basic quant + qual hybrid analysis
  • Ensured surveys were mobile-optimized and respondent-friendly for broader reach

Impact:

  • Delivered quick-turn insights that guided brand naming decisions with consumer validation
  • Reduced decision-making time through cost-efficient, scalable research
  • Helped stakeholders make confident calls on branding, messaging, and creative direction
  • Enabled agile testing culture across teams—supporting faster go-to-market readiness
