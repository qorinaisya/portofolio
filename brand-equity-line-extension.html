Brand Equity & Line Extension – Qualitative Exploration

Context: A brand aimed to strengthen its equity while exploring opportunities for line extension. The key challenge was understanding how consumers perceive the core brand and how far it could stretch into new spaces without losing relevance or trust.

Solution: Led a qualitative study using in-depth interviews and group discussions to explore:

  • Current brand perceptions, emotional associations, and trust drivers
  • Fit and stretch of potential line extensions—across categories and usage moments
  • Consumer reactions to product concepts, naming, and positioning ideas
  • Explored brand boundaries and opportunities for equity amplification

Impact:

  • Clarified the brand’s core equity pillars and areas of strength
  • Identified viable extension spaces that felt natural and credible to consumers
  • Provided clear guidance on positioning and messaging for new product launches
  • Informed innovation teams on how to expand the portfolio without diluting brand equity
