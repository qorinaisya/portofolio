Price Sensitivity Analysis – RMS Data + Python

Context: To support pricing strategy, we needed to evaluate how price changes impacted sales volume and market share using Nielsen Retail Measurement (RMS) data.

Solution: Conducted a price sensitivity analysis using Python and Nielsen RMS data:

  • Cleaned and structured multi-period sales data across brands, SKUs, and channels
  • Modeled price elasticity using regression techniques and cross-price elasticity for competitors
  • Delivered easy-to-use outputs for both technical and non-technical stakeholders

Impact:

  • Identified price thresholds that maximized volume and margin
  • Provided data-driven input for promotional planning and pricing decisions
  • Enabled cross-functional teams to simulate "what-if" scenarios using elasticity models
  • Supported pricing strategy optimization across key SKUs and retailers
