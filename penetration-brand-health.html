Unlocking Growth Through Penetration & Brand Health Study

Category: Health Supplements (Multivitamins, Skin, Cold & Flu)

Context: Needed a deep understanding of user & non-user behavior and brand performance to drive growth in a highly competitive market.

Solution:

  • Penetration Study: Mapped market segments, identified barriers among non-users, and uncovered unmet needs.
  • Brand Health Tracking: Assessed brand vs. competitors, analyzed brand drivers, and focused on key segments like female skin supplement users.

Impact:

  • Discovered high-opportunity segments for innovation
  • Informed targeted marketing strategies
  • Sharpened brand positioning with competitor insights
  • Aligned teams with clear, data-driven growth direction
