Retail Audit Analysis – Nielsen RMS

Context: To monitor brand performance and market dynamics, we used Nielsen RMS data to track sell-out trends, competitive movements, and retail execution across key categories and channels.

Solution: Led regular analysis and reporting of Retail Measurement Services (RMS) data from Nielsen:

  • Tracked volume and value share, distribution metrics, and pricing movements
  • Benchmarked brand vs. competitors across modern trade and general trade
  • Identified growth drivers, performance dips, and retail execution gaps
  • Translated data into actionable insights for marketing, sales, and trade strategy teams

Impact:

  • Delivered data-backed recommendations for channel strategy and promo optimization
  • Informed brand health diagnostics and competitive positioning
  • Supported cross-functional decision-making with clear, timely market intelligence
  • Contributed to market share growth by identifying actionable retail opportunities
