Sales Data Analysis – Sell-In (SI) & Sell-Out (SO)

Context: To optimize business performance, we analyzed Sell-In (SI) and Sell-Out (SO) data to monitor demand patterns, identify gaps, and support commercial decision-making across channels and partners.

Solution: Led structured analysis of internal and external sales data:

  • Tracked SI performance across distributors and regions
  • Analyzed SO trends at outlet level to understand true consumer pull
  • Conducted gap analysis between SI and SO to flag stock issues, slow movers, and supply chain misalignment
  • Visualized data into actionable dashboards and summaries for commercial teams

Impact:

  • Identified sales anomalies and opportunity areas by bridging SI & SO data
  • Informed demand planning, inventory management, and channel strategy
  • Enabled proactive interventions with distributors and sales teams
  • Supported revenue growth and operational efficiency through data-driven decisions
